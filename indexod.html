A long time ago, in a galaxy far,

It is a period of technological war. Rebel engineers, striking from a new IIT, have won their first victory against the enormous Technological Empire.

During the battle, Rebel spies managed to steal secret plans to the Empire's ultimate weapon, the Technological Fest, a fully loaded tech extravaganza with enough power to enlighten an entire planet.

Pursued by the Empire's sinister agents, these Engineers race home to their campus, custodian of the stolen plan that can save the people and restore the culture of science and technology in the college....
